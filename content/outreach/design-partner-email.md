## Slightly longer email

Hi [Name],

I’m building Corentis Shield to help regulated teams test AI-assisted workflows before they go live.

The product is designed to sit at the action boundary: where an AI-generated reply, recommendation or case update is about to become a customer message, record update or workflow step.

A design partner pilot could start in a non-production setting and test whether:

- policies can be turned into operational controls,
- risky outputs pause before action,
- human review is routed clearly,
- escalation points are visible,
- evidence is captured as the workflow runs.

Would you be open to a practical conversation about one workflow?
