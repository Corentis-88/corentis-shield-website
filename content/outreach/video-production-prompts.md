# Video production prompts

These prompts are for external video tools. Upload current website screenshots, product illustrations and PDF cover pages manually if the tool supports references.

## 30-second product video

Visual style: premium dark enterprise SaaS, calm cyan accents, realistic regulated workflow feel.

Tone: calm authority, not scareware.

Voiceover direction: natural UK business voice, clear and measured.

On-screen text: “AI needs a checkpoint before it acts.” “Check before action.” “Human review where it matters.” “Evidence for every decision.”

Key scenes: AI output moving toward customer workflow; Corentis checkpoint pauses risky output; human review card; evidence log; CTA.

Reference assets: homepage checkpoint visual, Runtime Checkpoint Explainer PDF, Corentis logo.

CTA: “Request a walkthrough.”

## 60-second product video

Visual style: premium website-style motion graphics mixed with warm customer-operations imagery.

Tone: practical, serious, approachable.

Voiceover direction: explain the problem simply before introducing Corentis Shield.

On-screen text: “If it can act, it needs a checkpoint.” “Proceed. Review. Escalate. Block.”

Key scenes: AI adoption; regulated workflow; risky output; Corentis check; review queue; evidence pack; pilot report.

Reference assets: /walkthrough, /methodology, Sample Pilot Report PDF.

CTA: “Explore the design partner pilot.”

## 90-second investor video

Visual style: premium infrastructure company, dark clean backgrounds, evidence cards, restrained motion.

Tone: confident and early-stage, no hype.

Voiceover direction: focus on timing, wedge, platform logic and what Corentis is seeking.

On-screen text: “AI agents need control infrastructure.” “Financial services complaints are the first wedge.” “Reusable checkpoint patterns.”

Key scenes: AI adoption trend; complaints/vulnerability wedge; checkpoint layer; scenario library; evidence packs; investor CTA.

Reference assets: Investor Overview PDF, /investors, /partners-and-funders.

CTA: “Start an investor/funder conversation.”

## 90-second design partner video

Visual style: human operations and pilot workflow, warm but premium.

Tone: collaborative and low-friction.

On-screen text: “Start with one workflow.” “Test before live use.” “Review where it matters.”

Key scenes: workflow selected; policy controls mapped; scenarios run; checkpoint simulation; review queue; evidence pack.

Reference assets: Design Partner Pack PDF, /design-partners, /pilot-report.

CTA: “Start a design partner conversation.”
