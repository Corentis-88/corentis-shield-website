# Spoken pitches

## 10-second pitch

Corentis Shield is an AI checkpoint for regulated workflows. It checks AI outputs before they become customer messages, case updates or workflow actions.

## 30-second pitch

Corentis Shield helps regulated teams use AI with more control. As AI agents start drafting replies, updating records and recommending actions, teams need a checkpoint before those outputs reach customers or systems. Corentis checks policy, risk, review and evidence requirements so sensitive actions can proceed, pause, escalate or be blocked before action.

## 60-second pitch

Corentis Shield is an AI checkpoint for regulated workflows. The problem is that AI is moving from answering questions to taking action: drafting replies, updating records, recommending next steps and triggering workflows. In regulated environments, that creates a control problem. Who checks the AI output before it affects a customer, case or live system? Corentis is designed to sit at that action boundary. It checks proposed outputs against policy, risk rules, human-review requirements and evidence requirements. The first wedge is financial-services complaints and vulnerable-customer workflows, because those workflows combine sensitivity, evidence needs and operational pressure.

## 90-second investor pitch

Corentis is an early-stage UK AI control company building Corentis Shield, an AI checkpoint for regulated workflows. AI adoption is moving from chat into operational action. In regulated environments, the risk is not only bad text. It is unchecked action: a customer message, case update, recommendation or workflow step moving forward without clear controls. Corentis starts with financial-services complaints and vulnerable-customer workflows as a narrow, high-consequence wedge. The broader platform path is policy controls, runtime checkpoints, scenario testing, review queues, evidence vaults and pilot reports. The opportunity is controlled AI action, not another chatbot.

## 90-second design partner pitch

Corentis Shield is designed for regulated teams who want to test AI-assisted workflows before production deployment. A design partner pilot can start with one sensitive workflow, such as complaints or vulnerable-customer handling. We map the workflow boundaries, translate policy into practical controls, run realistic scenarios, simulate runtime checkpoints, route review where needed and produce an evidence pack. The goal is not to claim live deployment or regulatory approval. The goal is to learn whether AI-assisted work can be controlled, reviewed and evidenced before wider rollout.

## 90-second assurance/governance pitch

Corentis Shield helps teams move from policy intent to runtime evidence. Policies and governance frameworks matter, but assurance teams also need to know what happened when AI-assisted workflows actually ran. What did the AI propose? Which control operated? Why was a sensitive action paused? Who reviewed it? What evidence was captured? Corentis is designed to create a checkpoint before action, route human review where it matters and produce artefacts such as control matrices, scenario logs, review histories and pilot reports.
