# First video build plan

## 1. Recommended first video

Create a 60-second product overview.

## 2. Goal

Explain Corentis Shield simply and make the right viewer want to click through to the website,
walkthrough, resources or contact page.

## 3. Format

- 16:9 website video.
- Optional 9:16 LinkedIn/social cutdown.
- Voiceover version first.
- Captions included.
- Calm modern music.

## 4. Scene-by-scene plan

- 0-5s: problem hook.
- 5-12s: AI checkpoint concept.
- 12-25s: regulated complaints/vulnerable-customer example.
- 25-38s: risky output paused and human review routed.
- 38-50s: evidence pack and pilot report.
- 50-60s: CTA.

## 5. Screen assets to capture

- Homepage hero.
- Methodology checkpoint graphic.
- Pilot report preview.
- Resources/PDF pack page.
- Contact or walkthrough page.

## 6. Voiceover script

AI is moving from chat into action. In regulated workflows, that creates a practical question:
what checks the output before it reaches a customer, case or system?

Corentis Shield is an AI checkpoint for regulated workflows.

It is designed to help teams test whether sensitive AI-assisted work can be checked before action,
routed to human review where it matters, and evidenced as the workflow runs.

Start with one workflow, such as complaints or vulnerable-customer handling. Test scenarios, pause
risky outputs, route review and generate a pilot evidence pack.

Corentis does not ask teams to blindly trust AI. It gives them a checkpoint before AI acts.

## 7. On-screen text

- AI is moving from chat to action.
- Regulated workflows need control before action.
- Corentis Shield checks proposed outputs.
- Risky outputs pause.
- Human review is routed.
- Evidence is captured.
- Start with one workflow.
- Request a walkthrough.

## 8. Music direction

Use subtle, calm, modern background music. Avoid dramatic cybersecurity music or anything that
makes the product feel fear-led.

## 9. Export settings

- 1080p minimum.
- 16:9 master.
- Optional 9:16 social cutdown.
- Captions burned in or supplied as a separate caption file.
- Export one voiceover version and one silent captioned version.

## 10. Acceptance criteria

- Understandable without sound.
- No fake customers or logos.
- No regulatory approval implication.
- No unsupported ROI.
- Clear CTA in the final five seconds.
- Corentis Shield positioning is clear within the first 10 seconds.
