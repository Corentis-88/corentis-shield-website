# Video execution pack

Tool-agnostic notes for Veo, Runway, Synthesia, VEED, Canva, CapCut, or screen recording plus voiceover.

Do not show fake customers, bank logos, regulated documents, third-party brands or regulatory approval. Keep the video focused on the problem, checkpoint concept, sample workflow, evidence pack and request for conversations.

## 1. 30-second product video script

Voiceover:

AI is moving from chat into action. In regulated workflows, that creates a new question: who checks what AI is about to do before it reaches a customer, case or system? Corentis Shield is an AI checkpoint for regulated workflows. It helps teams pause risky outputs, route human review and record evidence before sensitive actions move forward.

CTA: Request a walkthrough.

## 2. 60-second product video script

Voiceover:

AI agents can draft replies, recommend next steps, update records and trigger workflows. That can help teams move faster, but in regulated environments, unchecked outputs can create customer, operational and evidence risk. Corentis Shield sits at the action boundary. It checks proposed AI outputs against policy, risk rules, review requirements and evidence needs. Safe actions can continue. Sensitive actions can pause. Human review can be routed. Evidence can be captured as the workflow runs. Start with one workflow, test before live use and see where the checkpoint is needed.

CTA: Explore the design partner pilot.

## 3. 90-second investor video script

Voiceover:

Corentis is an early-stage UK AI control company building Corentis Shield, an AI checkpoint for regulated workflows. AI adoption is moving from chat into operational action. In regulated environments, the risk is not only inaccurate text. It is unchecked action: a customer message, case update, recommendation or workflow step moving forward without clear controls. Corentis starts with financial-services complaints and vulnerable-customer workflows as a narrow, high-consequence wedge. The broader platform path is policy controls, runtime checkpoints, scenario testing, review queues, evidence vaults and pilot reports. The opportunity is controlled AI action, not another chatbot.

CTA: Start an investor/funder conversation.

## 4. 90-second design partner video script

Voiceover:

Corentis Shield is designed for regulated teams who want to test AI-assisted workflows before production deployment. A design partner pilot can start with one sensitive workflow, such as complaints or vulnerable-customer handling. We map boundaries, translate policy into practical controls, run realistic scenarios, simulate runtime checkpoints, route review where needed and produce an evidence pack. The goal is not to claim live deployment or regulatory approval. The goal is to learn whether AI-assisted work can be controlled, reviewed and evidenced before wider rollout.

CTA: Start a design partner conversation.
