## 4. LinkedIn launch post

AI is moving from answering questions to taking action.

That changes the risk in regulated workflows.

A draft reply is one thing. A reply sent to a vulnerable customer, a record updated without review, or a complaint closed without enough evidence is another.

That is why I'm building Corentis Shield: an AI checkpoint for regulated workflows.

The goal is simple: check before action, route human review where it matters, and record evidence as the workflow runs.

I'm speaking with design partners, AI assurance stakeholders and investors/funding partners who are thinking about controlled AI adoption in regulated workflows.

## Shorter LinkedIn alternate

If AI can act in a regulated workflow, it needs a checkpoint before that action reaches the real world.

I'm building Corentis Shield to help teams check AI outputs, route human review and capture evidence before sensitive actions move forward.

I'm open to design partner and investor/funder conversations.

## Softer founder/building-in-public version

I'm building Corentis around one belief:
