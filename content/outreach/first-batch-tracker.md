# First outreach batch tracker

Use this as a manual tracker. Do not add fake researched contacts.

Recommended first batch:

- 5 investor/funder messages
- 5 design partner messages
- 3 assurance/governance messages
- 1 LinkedIn launch post

## Suggested target categories

Investor/funder targets:

- UK AI/enterprise SaaS investors
- fintech/regtech investors
- AI assurance/governance investors
- regional/northern investment funds
- public innovation/funding contacts where appropriate

Design partner targets:

- challenger banks
- building societies
- fintech lenders
- complaints outsourcing providers
- regulated customer support teams
- vulnerability/customer-care leads
- AI transformation leads in financial services

Assurance/governance targets:

- AI governance consultancies
- compliance advisory firms
- operational risk leaders
- financial services assurance teams
- internal audit innovation teams
